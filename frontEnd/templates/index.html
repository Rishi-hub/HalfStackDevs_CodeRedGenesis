    5. Sustainability scores are produced by a weighted average of 5 metrics: Energy Utiization, Oil Spills, Water Usage, Waste Production, and Greenhouse Emissions. Each metric is calculated as having a score between 0-20 for any particular metric. Here, lower values indicate unsustainability, and higher values indicate sustainability. From this, the reciprocal of this value is taken, and we are given a decimal value as a score for the metric as a perentage. Utilizing a scientific weighing of sustainability measures we produce a weighted average for the final sustainability score.
